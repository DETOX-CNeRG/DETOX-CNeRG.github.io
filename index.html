About us

Hate alert is a small group of researchers at CNeRG Lab, IIT Kharagpur. Their vision is to bring civility in online conversations by building systems to analyse, detect and mitigate hate in online social media. Recently, they have published papers in top conferences like AAAI, WWW, ICWSM and WebSci. More about their work can be found here.
